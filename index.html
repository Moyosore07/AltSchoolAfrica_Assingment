About Me

Biography

Born and raised in the dynamic city of Lagos, Nigeria, on October 22, 2002, Udebhulu Gideon with the Student ID: ALT/SOE/024/0236 is a driven and technology-oriented individual. He began his educational journey at Kith & Kin Nursery and Kindergarten, followed by primary schooling at First Citizen. Subsequently, Gideon attended Babington Macaulay Junior Seminary before completing a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science.
With a strong foundation in computer science and a keen interest in frontend engineering, he is eager to leverage his skills to create exceptional digital experiences. His ability to solve problems, pay attention to detail, and collaborate effectively makes him a valuable asset to any development team.

Why I Joined Alt School

I joined AltSchool Africa to accelerate my career in frontend engineering. AltSchool’s reputation for providing industry-relevant skills and a strong community aligns perfectly with my career goals. I believe the program's practical approach and emphasis on project-based learning will equip me with the necessary tools to excel in the dynamic tech industry.
Additionally, the opportunity to collaborate with like-minded individuals and gain mentorship from experienced professionals is invaluable. I am confident that AltSchool Africa will provide the ideal platform for me to enhance my skills and launch a successful career.

Goals for the School of Engineering Program

My primary goal at AltSchool Africa is to master the art and science of frontend engineering. I aim to develop a deep understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and to become proficient in modern frontend frameworks like React, Typescript or Vue.js.
I aspire to build visually appealing, responsive, and high-performance user interfaces that deliver exceptional user experiences. Additionally, I seek to cultivate strong problem-solving, critical thinking, and collaboration skills to thrive in a dynamic development environment. Ultimately, I hope to emerge as a skilled frontend engineer capable of contributing meaningfully to innovative digital products.
